ABOUT BPO 360+

BPO 360+ is an AI-driven platform designed to address every stage of the outsourcing lifecycle.

1. Recruitment

Our AI-powered recruitment tools enable you to source and hire top-tier talent quickly and efficiently. BPO 360+ automates candidate filtering, streamlines interview scheduling, and provides predictive analytics that help you identify the best cultural and skill-based fit.

Rajiv

The first round of interview is scheduled, interviewed, and shortlisted by our digital human Rajiv.

This data-driven approach reduces time-to-hire, eliminates biases, and ensures you attract high-caliber professionals who will contribute to your long-term success.

2. Training

Upskilling and reskilling your workforce has never been easier. BPO 360+ offers a comprehensive training module:

Guru

Our digital human role player Guru trains with a combination of videos, animations, quizzes, podcasts, role play, and games (with leaderboards). It’s a total multimodal learning experience that uses interactive learning paths, personalized assessments, and AI-generated performance feedback. Whether you need onboarding programs for new hires or advanced skill development for existing teams, our platform ensures your employees are equipped to excel in a rapidly changing industry.

Rosie

Our digital human role player Rosie is designed to assess employee knowledge of critical product and process information. She leverages a comprehensive database of questions to evaluate user responses, providing immediate feedback and generating detailed performance reports for both individual users and management. This data-driven approach allows for continuous improvement in employee knowledge and ensures consistent adherence to best practices across the organization.

Alex

Our digital human role player Alex simulates realistic customer interactions with agents. By mimicking human behavior, including diverse personalities, emotions, and communication styles, Alex provides a dynamic and challenging environment for agents to practice and improve their customer service skills. Through these simulated interactions, Alex evaluates agent performance across various dimensions, such as communication effectiveness, problem-solving abilities, and adherence to customer service protocols. This valuable feedback allows agents to identify areas for improvement and enhance their overall customer service experience.

3. Business Processes

BPO 360+ analyzes workflows, identifies bottlenecks, and optimizes processes to reduce overhead and enhance productivity. From order management and customer support to finance and compliance, our proven methodologies ensure consistent, high-quality output that drives sustainable growth.

4. Operations

Ensure smooth and efficient day-to-day operations with BPO 360+’s real-time monitoring and reporting tools. Gain complete visibility into performance metrics, resource allocation, and compliance status, all in one centralized platform. By automating routine tasks and providing actionable data, we help your teams focus on strategic objectives, improve service delivery, and stay ahead of the competition.

  • Comprehensive, End-to-End Solutions: From Recruitment to Operations, our platform covers every facet of BPO, reducing complexity and eliminating the need for multiple vendors.

  • AI-Powered Efficiency: By leveraging advanced analytics, process automation, and data-driven insights, we aim to enhance accuracy, reduce costs, and accelerate turnaround times.
